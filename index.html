Rhino Splits Case Study

Overview

Rhino Splits is a digital wallet application that facilitates peer-to-peer transactions.

Business Criteria

The main goals for Rhino Splits was for the term "Rhino Split" to become a verb as the result of creating a successful peer-to-peer payment application.

Constraints

Though there was no financial boundary for this project, I had a time constraint of 15-weeks to solve this problem.

Research

Competitor Analysis

Venmo
Strengths:
  • Customers love the ease-of-use and the QR code feature.
Weaknesses:
  • Venmo lacks budgeting features.
  • There's not a great way to organize/categorize transactions and it also only captures the transactions to and from the application.
Mint
Strengths:
  • Full service and easy-to-use personal finance tracking app.
Weaknesses:
  • Features for future planning are lacking.
  • Unable to differentiate individual digital wallet transactions.

Interview Process

In my first round of interviews, I conducted all the interviews over the phone. This make for a very awkward interview process. I also found that my questions were not worded in a way that encouraged conversation.

I didn't feel that I had gained any information that I could use so I decided to rework my questions, slightly change my ideal persona, and schedule some in person interviews.

My second round of interviews consisted of a group interview. It ended up being a lot of fun and very informative. Participants talked amongst themselves regarding the topics and I gained much more insight. The group dynamic felt more relaxed and the participants were more open to conversation. I liked the flexible feel of this second round of interviews. I gained more insight from the conversations that were naturally sparked than from the direct responses to my predetermined questions.

Predetermined Questions
  • What digital wallet apps do you currently use?
  • Are there any features in these apps that stand out to you?
  • Are there any downfalls with the apps you use?
  • Are there any features that you would like to see?

Insights

Venmo was by-far the most used digital wallet app among the interviewees.

The interviewees loved the QR feature within Venmo.

The majority of current digital wallet use is for peer-to-peer transactions.

I found that an overwhelming amount of Venmo users do not like the social media feed feature that the app offers.

There is a demand for budgeting integration in digial wallet use. If implemented, this would greatly expand the Rhino Splits user audience.

Persona

Based on my interviews, I found that the type of user that would benefit from a digital wallet app with a personal finance feature would likely be a younger adult who does not already have a robust personal finance plan in place.

Journey Map

I created a user journey to envision the expectations, thoughts, feelings, and actions as she goes through various steps of discovering Rhino Splits, setting up an account, and using the application.

I spent time considering opportunities for Rhino Splits to succeed with each step. For example, the onboarding/setting up process can often be a drag and a hassle. Knowing this, Rhino Splits has the opportunity to make the account set up process easy and engaging.

Lessons Learned

The research process is so important! Do not skimp!

I learned that insights gained within interviews are crucial to the entire process. It's important spend the time formulating questions that lead to engaging conversations. And it's okay to redo interviews if you feel that you didn't get enough information on the first try!
